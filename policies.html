Policies by Dr. Manmohan Singh

Policies by Manmohan Singh: Manmohan Singh's policies focused on economic growth, social welfare, and governance reforms. Key initiatives included economic liberalization in 1991, which opened India to global markets, and the implementation of MGNREGA for rural employment. His government enacted the Right to Information Act to enhance transparency and the Right to Education Act to make education a fundamental right. Singh prioritized healthcare with the National Rural Health Mission and signed the Indo-US Nuclear Deal for energy security. Additionally, he promoted fiscal discipline through the FRBM Act and prepared the foundation for the Goods and Services Tax (GST).
