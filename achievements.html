Dr. Manmohan Singh’s achievements

Manmohan Singh's tenure as Prime Minister and his earlier role as Finance Minister were marked by transformative achievements. He spearheaded India’s economic liberalization in 1991, opening the economy to global markets. As PM, he implemented landmark initiatives like the MGNREGA for rural employment, the RTI Act for transparency, and the Right to Education Act, ensuring free education for children. His government launched the National Rural Health Mission to improve healthcare and signed the historic Indo-US Nuclear Deal for energy security. Singh also laid the groundwork for the Goods and Services Tax (GST) and maintained a steady economic growth trajectory during his leadership.
